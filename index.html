Background
The Silent Crisis

Tropical Cyclone Senyar (Nov 2025) killed 1,207 people across Sumatra — destroying 301,012 homes and displacing 117,000+ residents. BMKG predicted it 8 days in advance, yet warnings never reached affected communities.

Over 40% of countries worldwide lack adequate early warning systems. Conventional EWS costs IDR 750 million per point — beyond reach of most vulnerable regions.

This is not a natural failure — it's a system failure. The technology to prevent it already exists. What's missing is affordability and access.

System Architecture
How AQUA-SENSE Works
1
IoT Field Sensors — Water level, flow rate, rainfall & soil moisture every 30 seconds.
2
LoRaWAN Network — 15 km wireless range. No cell towers or electricity needed.
3
AI & Cloud Processing — Machine learning predicts flooding up to 6 hours ahead.
4
3-Level Auto Alert — SMS blast, physical sirens & radio broadcast. No smartphone needed.
5
Cost: IDR 75M/point — 10× cheaper than conventional. Replicable across ASEAN.
